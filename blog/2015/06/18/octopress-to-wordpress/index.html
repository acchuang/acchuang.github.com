  1. 首先新增一rss.xml到source中

layout: nil

<?xml version=“1.0” encoding=“UTF-8”?> <atom:link rel=“hub” href=“http://pubsubhubbub.appspot.com” xmlns:atom=“http://www.w3.org/2005/Atom”/> {{ site.title | xml_escape }} Octopress {{ site.url }}

{% for post in site.posts limit: 20 %}
<item>
  <title>{{ post.title | xml_escape }}</title>
  <description>{{ post.content | expand_urls: site.url | cdata_escape }}</description>
  <link>{{ site.url }}{{ post.url }}</link>
  <pubDate>{{ post.date | date_to_xmlschema }}</pubDate>
  <guid>{{ site.url }}{{ post.id }}</guid>
</item>
{% endfor %}

原出處:http://5minutenpause.com/blog/2012/06/08/rss-finally-working-again/[https://gist.githubusercontent.com/5minpause/2896245/raw/rss.xml] 2.
